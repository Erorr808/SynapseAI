# Ancient Rome: Empire and Civilization

Ancient Rome represents one of history's greatest civilizations, building an empire that spanned three continents and establishing foundations for Western law, governance, and culture.

## Historical Periods

### The Kingdom (753-509 BCE)
- Traditional founding date: 753 BCE
- Monarchy government
- Expansion of territory
- Etruscan influence
- Early republican ideas

### The Republic (509-27 BCE)
- Overthrow of monarchy
- Dual consulate leadership
- Senate governmental body
- Expansion of territory
- Internal conflict periods

### The Empire (27 BCE-476 CE)
- Augustus first emperor
- Imperial stability
- Expansion peaks
- Economic prosperity
- Cultural flourishing

### Decline and Fall (264-476 CE)
- Military pressures
- Economic troubles
- Political instability
- Barbarian invasions
- Western empire collapse
