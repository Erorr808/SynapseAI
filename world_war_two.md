# World War II: Global Conflict and Transformation

World War II (1939-1945) was history's deadliest and most widespread international conflict, involving most nations and resulting in massive loss of life and complete geopolitical restructuring.

### Rise of Fascism

- Adolf Hitler's Nazi Germany
- Benito Mussolini's Italy
- Militaristic expansion
- Extreme nationalism and racism

### Prewar Expansion
- Germany remilitarizes Rhineland
- Anschluss: Austria annexation
- Sudetenland occupation
- Czechoslovakia conquest
- Appeasement policy failure

### Japan's Aggression
- Invasion of Manchuria
- Chinese invasion and occupation
- Southeast Asian expansion

### Soviet Union's Position
- Non-aggression pact with Germany
- Territorial gains in Poland
- Baltic state occupation
- Eventually becomes target
