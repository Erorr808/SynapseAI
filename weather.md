# Weather and Meteorology

Weather is the state of the atmosphere at a specific time and place, involving temperature, precipitation, wind, and atmospheric conditions.

## Atmospheric Structure

### Troposphere
- **Height**: 0-12 km (varies by latitude)
- **Temperature**: Decreases with altitude (-6.5°C/km)
- **Composition**: 75% of atmospheric mass
- **Weather**: All weather phenomena occur here
- **Features**: Weather systems, clouds, precipitation

### Stratosphere
- **Height**: 12-50 km
- **Temperature**: Increases with altitude (ozone layer)
- **Composition**: Ozone concentration peak
- **Features**: Jet streams, ozone depletion
- **Aircraft**: Commercial flight corridor

### Mesosphere
- **Height**: 50-85 km
- **Temperature**: Decreases to -90°C
- **Features**: Meteors burn up here
- **Composition**: Very thin air
- **Research**: Rocket studies, noctilucent clouds

### Thermosphere
- **Height**: 85-600 km
- **Temperature**: Increases to 1,500°C
- **Features**: Aurora borealis, satellites
- **Composition**: Ionized particles
- **Research**: Space weather, radio propagation

### Exosphere
- **Height**: 600 km and above
- **Temperature**: Very hot, diffuse boundary
- **Features**: Space begins here
- **Composition**: Scattered hydrogen atoms
- **Transition**: Atmosphere merges with space

## Weather Elements

### Temperature
- **Measurement**: Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin
- **Diurnal Variation**: Daily temperature changes
- **Seasonal Variation**: Annual temperature cycles
- **Heat Index**: Feels-like temperature
- **Wind Chill**: Cooling effect of wind

### Humidity
- **Absolute Humidity**: Water vapor mass per air volume
- **Relative Humidity**: Percentage of saturation
- **Dew Point**: Temperature for condensation
- **Specific Humidity**: Water vapor mass per dry air mass
- **Mixing Ratio**: Water vapor to dry air ratio

### Pressure
- **Atmospheric Pressure**: Air weight above surface
- **Barometric Pressure**: Surface pressure measurement
- **High Pressure**: Fair weather systems
- **Low Pressure**: Storm development
- **Pressure Gradient**: Wind driving force

### Wind
- **Speed Measurement**: Knots, mph, km/h, m/s
- **Direction**: From which wind blows (compass)
- **Gusts**: Sudden wind speed increases
- **Jet Streams**: High-altitude fast winds
- **Microclimates**: Local wind patterns

### Precipitation
- **Rain**: Liquid water droplets
- **Snow**: Ice crystals
- **Sleet**: Frozen rain droplets
- **Hail**: Layered ice pellets
- **Drizzle**: Fine rain droplets

## Weather Systems

### Air Masses
- **Continental Polar**: Cold, dry (cP)
- **Continental Tropical**: Hot, dry (cT)
- **Maritime Polar**: Cool, moist (mP)
- **Maritime Tropical**: Warm, moist (mT)
- **Arctic/Antarctic**: Extremely cold (A)

### Fronts
- **Cold Front**: Cold air advances on warm air
- **Warm Front**: Warm air advances on cold air
- **Stationary Front**: No movement between air masses
- **Occluded Front**: Cold front overtakes warm front
- **Dry Line**: Boundary between moist and dry air

### Cyclones and Anticyclones
- **Extratropical Cyclone**: Low pressure storm system
- **Tropical Cyclone**: Hurricane, typhoon, cyclone
- **Anticyclone**: High pressure system
- **Polar Low**: Small arctic storm
- **Mesocyclone**: Rotating thunderstorm updraft

### Thunderstorms
- **Single Cell**: Short-lived, isolated
- **Multi-Cell**: Cluster of cells
- **Squall Line**: Linear arrangement
- **Supercell**: Long-lived, severe
- **Derecho**: Widespread damaging winds

## Severe Weather

### Hurricanes/Typhoons/Cyclones
- **Formation**: Warm ocean temperatures (>26.5°C)
- **Structure**: Eye, eyewall, rainbands
- **Categories**: Saffir-Simpson scale (1-5)
- **Impacts**: Wind, storm surge, flooding
- **Tracks**: Generally westward, poleward curves

### Tornadoes
- **Formation**: Supercell thunderstorms
- **Structure**: Funnel cloud to ground
- **Rating**: Enhanced Fujita scale (EF0-EF5)
- **Tornado Alley**: Central US high frequency
- **Waterspouts**: Over water tornadoes

### Winter Storms
- **Blizzards**: High winds, heavy snow
- **Ice Storms**: Freezing rain accumulation
- **Lake Effect Snow**: Downwind snow bands
- **Nor'easters**: East coast winter storms
- **Alberta Clippers**: Fast-moving cold fronts

### Droughts and Heat Waves
- **Meteorological Drought**: Precipitation deficit
- **Agricultural Drought**: Soil moisture shortage
- **Hydrological Drought**: Water supply shortage
- **Heat Index**: Temperature + humidity
- **Urban Heat Islands**: City temperature elevation

## Climate vs Weather

### Weather
- **Time Scale**: Hours to days
- **Scope**: Local to regional
- **Predictability**: Hours to 10 days
- **Measurement**: Current conditions
- **Variability**: High short-term changes

### Climate
- **Time Scale**: 30+ years average
- **Scope**: Global to regional patterns
- **Predictability**: Long-term trends
- **Measurement**: Statistical averages
- **Variability**: Gradual long-term changes

### Climate Classification
- **Köppen System**: Temperature/precipitation based
- **Tropical**: Always warm, seasonal rainfall
- **Dry**: Arid and semi-arid regions
- **Temperate**: Four distinct seasons
- **Continental**: Large temperature ranges
- **Polar**: Extremely cold climates

## Weather Forecasting

### Observation Methods
- **Surface Stations**: Ground-based measurements
- **Weather Balloons**: Upper air data (radiosondes)
- **Weather Satellites**: Global coverage
- **Weather Radar**: Precipitation detection
- **Doppler Radar**: Wind speed/direction

### Forecasting Models
- **Numerical Weather Prediction**: Computer models
- **Ensemble Forecasting**: Multiple model runs
- **Statistical Models**: Historical pattern analysis
- **Analog Forecasting**: Similar past events
- **AI/ML Models**: Machine learning predictions

### Forecast Products
- **Short-Range**: 0-3 days, high accuracy
- **Medium-Range**: 3-10 days, good accuracy
- **Long-Range**: Monthly/seasonal, lower accuracy
- **Severe Weather**: Watches/warnings for hazards
- **Aviation Forecasts**: Specialized for flight

### Forecast Accuracy
- **Skill Scores**: Improvement over climatology
- **Bias Correction**: Systematic error adjustment
- **Uncertainty Quantification**: Confidence levels
- **Verification**: Forecast vs observation comparison
- **Model Improvement**: Continuous refinement

## Atmospheric Phenomena

### Clouds
- **Cumulus**: Puffy, fair weather
- **Stratus**: Layered, overcast
- **Cirrus**: High, wispy ice crystals
- **Nimbus**: Rain-producing (cumulonimbus)
- **Special Types**: Lenticular, mammatus, noctilucent

### Optical Phenomena
- **Rainbows**: Light refraction through raindrops
- **Halos**: Ice crystal light bending
- **Parhelia**: Sun dogs, 22° from sun
- **Mirages**: Light bending over hot surfaces
- **Auroras**: Solar particle atmospheric excitation

### Severe Weather Signals
- **Lightning**: Electrical discharge
- **Thunder**: Sonic shockwave
- **Tornado Sirens**: Emergency warnings
- **Flash Flood Warnings**: Rapid water rise
- **Heat Advisories**: Dangerous temperatures

## Weather Technology

### Remote Sensing
- **Satellite Imagery**: Visible, infrared, microwave
- **LIDAR**: Light detection and ranging
- **SODAR**: Sound detection and ranging
- **Radar Networks**: Weather surveillance
- **GPS Meteorology**: Water vapor measurement

### Communication Systems
- **NOAA Weather Radio**: Emergency broadcasts
- **Mobile Apps**: Real-time weather data
- **Social Media**: Storm reporting
- **Emergency Alert System**: Public warnings
- **Weather APIs**: Developer access

### Climate Monitoring
- **Global Climate Models**: Long-term predictions
- **Climate Reanalysis**: Historical reconstruction
- **Paleoclimate Data**: Proxy records
- **Carbon Cycle Monitoring**: Greenhouse gas tracking
- **Ocean-Atmosphere Coupling**: Climate system interactions

## Weather Impact on Society

### Economic Effects
- **Agriculture**: Crop yields, harvest timing
- **Transportation**: Flight delays, road conditions
- **Energy**: Power demand, renewable generation
- **Insurance**: Risk assessment, premiums
- **Tourism**: Seasonal weather-dependent

### Public Health
- **Heat-Related Illness**: Dehydration, heat stroke
- **Cold-Related Illness**: Hypothermia, frostbite
- **Air Quality**: Pollution dispersion
- **Disease Vectors**: Mosquito-borne illnesses
- **Mental Health**: Seasonal affective disorder

### Disaster Preparedness
- **Emergency Management**: Response planning
- **Infrastructure Design**: Weather-resistant building
- **Early Warning Systems**: Timely alerts
- **Community Resilience**: Adaptation strategies
- **Risk Communication**: Public education

Weather shapes daily life, influences economies, and drives technological innovation in prediction and response.
