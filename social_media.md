# Social Media and Digital Communication

Social media platforms enable user-generated content sharing, networking, and community building through digital technologies.

## Social Media Evolution

### Early Platforms (1990s-2000s)
- **Six Degrees** (1997): First social networking site
- **Friendster** (2002): Early social gaming integration
- **MySpace** (2003): Music and personalization focus
- **LinkedIn** (2003): Professional networking
- **Facebook** (2004): Campus network expansion
- **YouTube** (2005): Video sharing platform

### Web 2.0 Era (2005-2010)
- **Twitter** (2006): Microblogging and real-time updates
- **Tumblr** (2007): Blogging and content curation
- **Instagram** (2010): Photo and video sharing
- **Pinterest** (2010): Visual discovery and bookmarking
- **Snapchat** (2011): Ephemeral messaging

### Mobile-First Era (2010s-present)
- **TikTok** (2016): Short-form video content
- **Clubhouse** (2020): Audio-based social networking
- **Discord** (2015): Gaming community platform
- **Twitch** (2011): Live streaming platform
- **BeReal** (2020): Authentic moment sharing
