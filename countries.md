# Countries of the World

Countries are sovereign states with defined territories, governments, and populations.

## Major World Powers

### United States
- **Capital**: Washington, D.C.
- **Population**: 331 million
- **Government**: Federal presidential republic
- **Economy**: World's largest, technology and services
- **Geography**: 50 states, diverse landscapes
- **Culture**: Multicultural, Hollywood entertainment

### China
- **Capital**: Beijing
- **Population**: 1.4 billion
- **Government**: One-party communist state
- **Economy**: Manufacturing powerhouse, second largest GDP
- **Geography**: Vast territory, Himalayas to Pacific
- **Culture**: Ancient civilization, Mandarin language

### India
- **Capital**: New Delhi
- **Population**: 1.4 billion
- **Government**: Federal parliamentary republic
- **Economy**: IT services, agriculture, emerging markets
- **Geography**: Subcontinent, Himalayas, Ganges River
- **Culture**: Diverse religions, Bollywood, yoga

### Russia
- **Capital**: Moscow
- **Population**: 146 million
- **Government**: Federal semi-presidential republic
- **Economy**: Energy exports, military industry
- **Geography**: Largest country, 11 time zones
- **Culture**: Literature, ballet, Orthodox Christianity

### Japan
- **Capital**: Tokyo
- **Population**: 126 million
- **Government**: Constitutional monarchy
- **Economy**: Technology, automotive, electronics
- **Geography**: Island archipelago, Mount Fuji
- **Culture**: Tea ceremony, anime, samurai tradition

### Germany
- **Capital**: Berlin
- **Population**: 83 million
- **Government**: Federal parliamentary republic
- **Economy**: Engineering, automotive, EU leadership
- **Geography**: Central Europe, Alps, Rhine River
- **Culture**: Philosophy, beer, Oktoberfest

### United Kingdom
- **Capital**: London
- **Population**: 67 million
- **Government**: Constitutional monarchy
- **Economy**: Financial services, creative industries
- **Geography**: Island nation, varied landscapes
- **Culture**: Royal family, tea, Beatles music

### France
- **Capital**: Paris
- **Population**: 67 million
- **Government**: Unitary semi-presidential republic
- **Economy**: Fashion, wine, tourism
- **Geography**: Western Europe, Alps, Loire Valley
- **Culture**: Cuisine, art, intellectual tradition

## Emerging Economies

### Brazil
- **Capital**: Brasília
- **Population**: 213 million
- **Government**: Federal presidential republic
- **Economy**: Agriculture, mining, biofuels
- **Geography**: Amazon rainforest, beaches
- **Culture**: Carnival, soccer, Portuguese language

### South Korea
- **Capital**: Seoul
- **Population**: 52 million
- **Government**: Presidential republic
- **Economy**: Technology, shipbuilding, K-pop
- **Geography**: Peninsula, mountains, DMZ
- **Culture**: Kimchi, hanbok, Confucian values

### Indonesia
- **Capital**: Jakarta
- **Population**: 273 million
- **Government**: Presidential republic
- **Economy**: Palm oil, tourism, natural resources
- **Geography**: 17,000 islands, volcanoes
- **Culture**: Diverse ethnic groups, gamelan music

### Mexico
- **Capital**: Mexico City
- **Population**: 128 million
- **Government**: Federal presidential republic
- **Economy**: Manufacturing, oil, remittances
- **Geography**: Sierra Madre mountains, Yucatan peninsula
- **Culture**: Aztec heritage, mariachi music, Day of the Dead

### Turkey
- **Capital**: Ankara
- **Population**: 84 million
- **Government**: Presidential republic
- **Economy**: Textiles, tourism, construction
- **Geography**: Bridges Europe and Asia
- **Culture**: Ottoman heritage, Turkish bath, kebab

## African Nations

### Nigeria
- **Capital**: Abuja
- **Population**: 218 million
- **Government**: Federal presidential republic
- **Economy**: Oil, agriculture, Nollywood
- **Geography**: Niger River, savannas
- **Culture**: 250+ ethnic groups, Afrobeat music

### Egypt
- **Capital**: Cairo
- **Population**: 104 million
- **Government**: Presidential republic
- **Economy**: Tourism, Suez Canal, agriculture
- **Geography**: Nile River, Sahara Desert
- **Culture**: Ancient pyramids, Arabic calligraphy

### South Africa
- **Capital**: Cape Town (legislative), Pretoria (administrative)
- **Population**: 60 million
- **Government**: Parliamentary republic
- **Economy**: Mining, tourism, wine
- **Geography**: Table Mountain, Kruger National Park
- **Culture**: Rainbow nation, ubuntu philosophy

### Kenya
- **Capital**: Nairobi
- **Population**: 54 million
- **Government**: Presidential republic
- **Economy**: Tourism, tea, technology hub
- **Geography**: Great Rift Valley, Maasai Mara
- **Culture**: Maasai warriors, safari tradition

### Israel

## Small but Notable Countries

### Netherlands

Countries represent diverse cultures, economies, and political systems across the global landscape.
