# Robotics and Automation

Robotics combines mechanical engineering, electronics, computer science, and AI to create machines that perform tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously.

## Robotics Fundamentals

### Robot Components
- **Actuators**: Motors, hydraulics, pneumatics for movement
- **Sensors**: Vision, touch, proximity, force detection
- **Controllers**: Microcontrollers, PLCs, computers for control
- **End Effectors**: Grippers, tools, manipulators
- **Power Systems**: Batteries, fuel cells, wired power

### Robot Types
- **Manipulator Arms**: Industrial assembly robots
- **Mobile Robots**: Wheeled, tracked, legged locomotion
- **Humanoid Robots**: Human-like form and movement
- **Service Robots**: Domestic and commercial assistance
- **Micro/Nanorobots**: Miniature scale operations

### Control Systems
- **Open-Loop Control**: Pre-programmed movements
- **Closed-Loop Control**: Feedback-based adjustment
- **PID Controllers**: Proportional-integral-derivative control
- **Adaptive Control**: Self-adjusting parameters
- **Learning Control**: AI-based optimization
