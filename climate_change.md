# Climate Change and Global Warming

Climate change represents one of humanity's most pressing challenges, driven by greenhouse gas emissions and causing widespread environmental and social impacts globally.

## Greenhouse Effect and Gases

### Natural Greenhouse Effect
- Solar radiation reaching Earth
- Atmospheric gases trapping heat
- Thermal radiation reradiation
- Temperature equilibrium maintenance
- Essential for life existence

### Anthropogenic Greenhouse Gases
- Carbon dioxide: Fossil fuel burning
- Methane: Livestock and decomposition
- Nitrous oxide: Agriculture and industrial
- Fluorinated gases: Refrigeration and industrial
- Ozone depletion layer separate issue

### Carbon Dioxide Concentration
- Historical levels measurement
- Pre-industrial 280 ppm
- Current 410+ ppm levels
- Increasing concentration
- Highest in millions of years
