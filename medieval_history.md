# Medieval History: The Middle Ages

The Medieval period (5th-15th centuries) represents a transformative era connecting classical antiquity to the modern world, characterized by feudalism, religious dominance, and gradual cultural development.

## Historical Periods

### Early Middle Ages (5th-10th centuries)
- Fall of Roman Empire
- Barbarian kingdoms rise
- Charlemagne's empire
- Viking invasions
- Feudalism emergence

### High Middle Ages (11th-13th centuries)
- Feudal system maturity
- Crusades religious wars
- Cathedral construction
- Scholastic philosophy
- University development

### Late Middle Ages (14th-15th centuries)
- 100 Years War
- Black Death plague
- Renaissance beginning
- Printing press invention
- Exploration era beginning
