# Sports and Recreation

Sports encompass physical activities, games, and competitions that promote physical fitness, skill development, and entertainment.

## Major Team Sports

### Football (Soccer)
- **Origins**: England, 19th century
- **Global Popularity**: most played sport worldwide
- **Major Tournaments**: World Cup, Champions League, Premier League
- **Skills**: dribbling, passing, shooting, goalkeeping
- **Positions**: striker, midfielder, defender, goalkeeper

### American Football
- **Structure**: NFL, college football, high school
- **Game Format**: 4 quarters, 60 minutes total
- **Positions**: quarterback, running back, wide receiver, lineman
- **Super Bowl**: annual championship game
- **Strategy**: offense vs defense, play calling

### Basketball
- **Inventor**: James Naismith, 1891
- **NBA**: professional league with global stars
- **Skills**: shooting, dribbling, passing, defense
- **Positions**: point guard, shooting guard, small forward, power forward, center
- **Olympic Sport**: men's and women's competitions

### Baseball
- **Origins**: United States, 19th century
- **MLB**: Major League Baseball
- **Game Structure**: 9 innings, pitcher vs batter
- **Positions**: pitcher, catcher, infield, outfield
- **World Series**: annual championship
- **Statistics**: batting average, ERA, home runs

### Hockey
- **Ice Hockey**: NHL, Olympic sport
- **Field Hockey**: international competition
- **Skills**: skating, stickhandling, shooting
- **Positions**: center, wing, defenseman, goalie
- **Stanley Cup**: NHL championship trophy

## Individual Sports

### Track and Field
- **Sprinting**: 100m, 200m, 400m dashes
- **Middle Distance**: 800m, 1500m, 3000m runs
- **Long Distance**: 5000m, 10000m, marathon
- **Field Events**: high jump, long jump, pole vault, shot put, discus, javelin
- **Hurdles**: 110m, 400m barriers

### Swimming
- **Strokes**: freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, butterfly
- **Distances**: 50m, 100m, 200m, 400m, 800m, 1500m
- **Events**: individual medley, relays
- **Open Water**: marathon swimming
- **Synchronized Swimming**: artistic routines

### Tennis
- **Court Types**: grass, clay, hard court
- **Grand Slams**: Wimbledon, French Open, US Open, Australian Open
- **Skills**: serve, groundstrokes, volleys, overheads
- **Formats**: singles, doubles, mixed doubles
- **Scoring**: love, 15, 30, 40, game, set, match

### Golf
- **Course Layout**: par 3, 4, 5 holes
- **Clubs**: woods, irons, wedges, putter
- **Scoring**: stroke play, match play
- **Majors**: Masters, PGA Championship, US Open, Open Championship
- **Skills**: driving, iron play, short game, putting

### Combat Sports
- **Boxing**: weight classes, rounds
- **Mixed Martial Arts (MMA)**: striking and grappling
- **Wrestling**: freestyle, Greco-Roman
- **Judo**: throwing techniques
- **Karate**: striking art
