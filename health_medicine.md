# Health and Medicine

Health encompasses physical, mental, and social well-being, while medicine focuses on preventing, diagnosing, and treating diseases.

## Anatomy and Physiology

### Human Body Systems
- **Skeletal System**: 206 bones, support, protection, movement
- **Muscular System**: voluntary and involuntary muscles
- **Cardiovascular System**: heart, blood vessels, circulation
- **Respiratory System**: lungs, gas exchange
- **Digestive System**: nutrient absorption and waste elimination
- **Nervous System**: brain, spinal cord, information processing
- **Endocrine System**: hormone regulation
- **Immune System**: defense against pathogens
- **Reproductive System**: reproduction and hormone production
- **Urinary System**: waste filtration and fluid balance

### Vital Signs
- **Heart Rate**: 60-100 bpm at rest
- **Blood Pressure**: systolic/diastolic (120/80 mmHg normal)
- **Respiratory Rate**: 12-20 breaths per minute
- **Body Temperature**: 98.6°F (37°C) average
- **Oxygen Saturation**: 95-100% normal

## Common Diseases and Conditions

### Cardiovascular Diseases
- **Coronary Artery Disease**: plaque buildup in arteries
- **Hypertension**: high blood pressure
- **Heart Failure**: weakened heart muscle
- **Stroke**: brain damage from blocked blood flow
- Risk factors: smoking, poor diet, lack of exercise, stress

### Respiratory Diseases
- **Asthma**: inflamed airways, difficulty breathing
- **Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)**: emphysema, chronic bronchitis
- **Pneumonia**: lung infection
- **Lung Cancer**: uncontrolled cell growth in lungs

### Metabolic Disorders
- **Diabetes Mellitus**: impaired insulin function
  - Type 1: autoimmune destruction of beta cells
  - Type 2: insulin resistance
- **Obesity**: excessive body fat
- **Metabolic Syndrome**: cluster of risk factors

### Infectious Diseases
- **Bacterial Infections**: strep throat, tuberculosis, urinary tract infections
- **Viral Infections**: influenza, HIV/AIDS, COVID-19
- **Fungal Infections**: athlete's foot, candidiasis
- **Parasitic Infections**: malaria, intestinal parasites

### Mental Health Disorders
- **Depression**: persistent sadness, loss of interest
- **Anxiety Disorders**: excessive worry, panic attacks
- **Bipolar Disorder**: mood swings between mania and depression
- **Schizophrenia**: distorted thinking and perception
- **Eating Disorders**: anorexia, bulimia, binge eating

### Cancer
- **Carcinogenesis**: initiation, promotion, progression
- **Types**: carcinoma, sarcoma, leukemia, lymphoma
- **Treatment**: surgery, chemotherapy, radiation, immunotherapy
- **Prevention**: screening, lifestyle changes

## Medical Specialties

### Primary Care
- **Family Medicine**: comprehensive care for all ages
- **Internal Medicine**: adult medical conditions
- **Pediatrics**: children's health
- **Obstetrics/Gynecology**: women's health and pregnancy

### Surgical Specialties
- **General Surgery**: abdominal and related procedures
- **Orthopedic Surgery**: bones, joints, muscles
- **Cardiac Surgery**: heart and blood vessels
- **Neurosurgery**: brain and nervous system

### Other Specialties
- **Dermatology**: skin conditions
- **Ophthalmology**: eye diseases
- **Otolaryngology**: ear, nose, throat
- **Psychiatry**: mental health
- **Radiology**: medical imaging
- **Pathology**: disease diagnosis
- **Anesthesiology**: pain management and anesthesia

## Diagnostic Methods

### Laboratory Tests
- **Blood Tests**: CBC, metabolic panel, lipid profile
- **Urine Tests**: urinalysis, culture
- **Microbiology**: cultures, PCR testing
- **Immunology**: antibody tests, allergy testing

### Imaging Studies
- **X-rays**: bone fractures, chest imaging
- **CT Scans**: detailed cross-sectional images
- **MRI**: soft tissue imaging, brain scans
- **Ultrasound**: real-time imaging, pregnancy monitoring
- **Nuclear Medicine**: functional imaging

### Other Diagnostics
- **Electrocardiogram (ECG)**: heart electrical activity
- **Endoscopy**: internal organ visualization
- **Biopsy**: tissue sampling
- **Genetic Testing**: hereditary conditions

## Treatment Approaches

### Pharmacological
- **Drug Classes**: analgesics, antibiotics, antihypertensives
- **Drug Development**: preclinical, clinical trials, FDA approval
- **Pharmacokinetics**: absorption, distribution, metabolism, excretion
- **Drug Interactions**: synergistic, antagonistic effects

### Surgical Interventions
- **Minimally Invasive**: laparoscopy, endoscopy
- **Open Surgery**: traditional incisions
- **Robotic Surgery**: precision procedures
- **Transplant Surgery**: organ replacement

### Alternative Therapies
- **Physical Therapy**: rehabilitation and mobility
- **Occupational Therapy**: daily living skills
- **Acupuncture**: traditional Chinese medicine
- **Herbal Medicine**: plant-based treatments
- **Mindfulness**: meditation and stress reduction

Health and medicine combine science, compassion, and ethics to improve human well-being.
