# Cooking and Cuisine

Cooking is the art and science of preparing food through various methods and techniques.

## Cooking Methods

### Dry Heat Cooking
- **Baking**: Oven heat, even cooking, Maillard reaction
- **Roasting**: High heat, concentrates flavors, crispy exterior
- **Broiling**: Direct heat from above, quick cooking
- **Grilling**: Open flame, smoky flavor, char marks
- **Sautéing**: High heat, small amount of fat, quick cooking
- **Pan-frying**: Moderate heat, more fat, crispy exterior

### Moist Heat Cooking
- **Boiling**: Water at 100°C, gentle cooking, retains nutrients
- **Steaming**: Water vapor, healthy, retains vitamins
- **Poaching**: Gentle simmering in liquid, delicate foods
- **Simmering**: Below boiling, tenderizes tough cuts
- **Braising**: Browning then slow cooking in liquid
- **Stewing**: Long slow cooking in liquid, fall-apart tender

### Other Techniques
- **Frying**: Deep frying in hot oil, crispy exterior
- **Microwaving**: Electromagnetic waves, quick heating
- **Sous-vide**: Vacuum-sealed, precise temperature control
- **Pressure cooking**: High pressure, faster cooking
- **Slow cooking**: Low and slow, tender results

## World Cuisines

### Italian Cuisine
- **Pasta**: Various shapes, sauces, fresh ingredients
- **Pizza**: Neapolitan, Roman, thin crust variations
- **Risotto**: Creamy rice dish, Arborio rice
- **Osso Buco**: Braised veal shanks, gremolata
- **Gelato**: Italian ice cream, lower fat content

### French Cuisine
- **Escargot**: Snails in garlic butter
- **Bouillabaisse**: Marseille fish stew
- **Coq au Vin**: Chicken in red wine
- **Boeuf Bourguignon**: Beef stew with wine
- **Crème Brûlée**: Custard with caramelized sugar

### Chinese Cuisine
- **Dim Sum**: Small plates, steamed dumplings
- **Peking Duck**: Crispy skin, thin pancakes
- **Kung Pao Chicken**: Spicy stir-fry with peanuts
- **Mapo Tofu**: Spicy tofu with ground meat
- **Hot Pot**: Communal cooking in broth

### Indian Cuisine
- **Curry**: Various spice blends, regional variations
- **Tandoori**: Yogurt-marinated, clay oven cooked
- **Biryani**: Layered rice dish with meat/spices
- **Dosa**: Fermented crepe, South Indian
- **Masala Chai**: Spiced tea with milk

### Mexican Cuisine
- **Tacos**: Soft/hard shells, various fillings
- **Enchiladas**: Rolled tortillas with sauce
- **Fajitas**: Sizzling grilled meat with peppers
- **Mole**: Complex sauce with chocolate
- **Ceviche**: Citrus-marinated seafood

### Japanese Cuisine
- **Sushi**: Vinegared rice with seafood
- **Sashimi**: Raw fish slices
- **Tempura**: Deep-fried battered vegetables/seafood
- **Ramen**: Noodle soup, various broths
- **Kaiseki**: Multi-course traditional meal

## Baking Fundamentals

### Flour and Leavening
- **All-purpose Flour**: Versatile, 10-12% protein
- **Bread Flour**: High protein, chewy texture
- **Cake Flour**: Low protein, tender crumb
- **Yeast**: Biological leavening, fermentation
- **Baking Powder**: Chemical leavening, double-acting
- **Baking Soda**: With acid, creates carbon dioxide

### Pastry Techniques
- **Shortcrust Pastry**: Flaky, butter-rich
- **Puff Pastry**: Many layers, laminated
- **Choux Pastry**: Light, hollow, cream puffs
- **Phyllo**: Thin sheets, crispy layers
- **Hot Water Crust**: Sturdy, raised pies

### Sweet Baking
- **Cookies**: Drop, rolled, bar varieties
- **Cakes**: Layer, bundt, sheet pans
- **Brownies**: Fudgy vs cakey
- **Muffins**: Quick bread, various flavors
- **Scones**: British quick bread, clotted cream

## Food Science

### Maillard Reaction
- **Browning**: Amino acids + sugars + heat
- **Flavor Development**: Complex taste compounds
- **Color Change**: Golden brown appearance
- **Texture**: Crispy exteriors

### Food Safety
- **Temperature Danger Zone**: 40-140°F bacterial growth
- **Cross-contamination**: Raw to cooked transfer
- **Proper Storage**: Refrigeration, freezing
- **Foodborne Illness**: Salmonella, E. coli prevention

### Nutrition in Cooking
- **Vitamin Retention**: Steaming preserves nutrients
- **Healthy Fats**: Olive oil, avocados
- **Protein Denaturation**: Heat changes protein structure
- **Fermentation**: Probiotics, flavor development
